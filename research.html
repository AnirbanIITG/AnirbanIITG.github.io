Research Experience

Sl. No. Research Topics
[1] QoE Aware Client-side Video Adaptation Algorithm for Cellular Networks

  • Two client-side Rate Adaptation models are proposed for the DASH (Dynamic Adaptive Streaming over HTTP) framework in maximizing the Quality of Experience (QoE) of the clients.

  • In the first approach Client-side Scalable Video Coding (SVC) based bitrate adaptation framework called Video Quality Adaptation Framework (VQAF) with two distinct phases called the Download and Smooth-out phases is proposed.

  • The Download Phase fetches new segments at selected quality levels until buffer size reaches a stipulated safety Threshold.

  • The Smooth-out Phase repeatedly upgrades a subset of the already downloaded segments by one enhancement level, until buffer size reduces below the safety Threshold.

  • In the next approach a Machine Learning based Rate Prediction model called the Video Bit-Rate Prediction Model (VBPM) using Long Short Term Memory (LSTM) for Adaptive Streaming over HTTP is proposed.
